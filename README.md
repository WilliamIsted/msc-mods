# MSC Mods

Releases and issue tracking for my My Summer Car / My Winter Car mods.

| Mod | Game | Latest |
|---|---|---|
| **Ghost Car** - record your races, then race your ghost | My Summer Car | [Releases](https://github.com/WilliamIsted/msc-mods/releases?q=GhostCar) |

## Bug reports

Open an [issue](https://github.com/WilliamIsted/msc-mods/issues) and name the mod in the title. Include your MSCLoader version and `output_log.txt` where possible.

## Installing a mod

Download the mod's zip from [Releases](https://github.com/WilliamIsted/msc-mods/releases) and drag its contents into your game's `Mods` folder (requires [MSCLoader](https://github.com/piotrulos/MSCLoader)).
