# MSC Mods

Releases and issue tracking for my My Summer Car mods.

| Mod | Description | Latest |
|---|---|---|
| **Ghost Car** | Record your races, then race your ghost | [Releases](https://github.com/WilliamIsted/msc-mods/releases?q=ghost-car) |
| **Fine Choke Control** | Scroll-wheel fine control for the Satsuma choke | [Releases](https://github.com/WilliamIsted/msc-mods/releases?q=fine-choke-control) |

## Bug reports

Open an [issue](https://github.com/WilliamIsted/msc-mods/issues) and name the mod in the title. Include your MSCLoader version and `output_log.txt` where possible.

## Installing a mod

Download the mod's zip from [Releases](https://github.com/WilliamIsted/msc-mods/releases) and drag its contents into your game's `Mods` folder (requires [MSCLoader](https://github.com/piotrulos/MSCLoader)).
